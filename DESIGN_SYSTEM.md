# Out in the Vines — Design System

## Brand character
Sophisticated wine country with queer warmth. Vinny is curious, witty, welcoming, and useful.

## Palette
- Deep plum `#4B1E4D`
- Grape purple `#7A2E6B`
- Teal `#0F7A6B`
- Aqua `#2DB4A3`
- Olive `#6F8E3A`
- Gold `#D4A23F`
- Cream `#F1E8D9`

## Typography
- Editorial display: Playfair Display
- Product interface: DM Sans
- Vinny moments: Sacramento or final custom script

## Interface rules
- Ratings always show status, confidence, and review date.
- Photography needs usage rights and meaningful alt text.
- Rainbow iridescence belongs primarily to Vinny.
- Mobile navigation stays limited to five principal destinations.
- My Day defaults to a maximum of three wineries.
