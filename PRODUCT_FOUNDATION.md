# Out in the Vines — Product Foundation v0.2

## Product promise
**Find great wine. Know where you will feel welcome. Let Vinny handle the details.**

## Phase 1 user jobs
1. Help me choose a winery quickly.
2. Tell me what the experience will feel like.
3. Explain the evidence behind its welcome rating.
4. Tell me what Andrew and Antonio actually order.
5. Let me save places and assemble a realistic three-stop day.

## Trust principles
- Inclusivity ratings are never sold.
- Sponsored placements are visibly labeled.
- A missing review is not a negative review.
- Public claims require a source and review date.
- Political giving or endorsements receive context and are never the sole rating input.
- Wineries receive a correction and response process.
- Operational facts need a last-verified date.
- Personal experience is labeled as personal experience.

## Release path
- v0.2: Visual prototype with five wineries
- v0.3: Final brand exports and owned/licensed winery images
- v0.4: Andrew and Antonio tasting notes
- v0.5: Research-backed welcome profiles
- v0.6: Real map, hours, and route logic
- v0.7: Accounts, cloud favorites, and saved trips
- v1.0: Public Temecula launch
