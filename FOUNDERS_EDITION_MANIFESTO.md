# Out in the Vines — Founder’s Edition Design Manifesto

1. Wine first.
2. Hospitality is the differentiator.
3. Vinny is the guide, not decoration.
4. Editorial over algorithm.
5. Every winery deserves its own personality.
6. Scan first. Read second.
7. Celebrate excellence.
8. Personal voices appear with restraint.
9. The Welcome Index™ is always visible, always explained, and never pay-to-play.
10. Premium means clarity, restraint, and trust.

- Out in the Vines: trusted guide and publication
- Welcome Index™: methodology and transparency framework
- Vinny Verified™: earned recognition
- Vinny: guide and concierge
